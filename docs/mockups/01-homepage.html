Design Decisions

  • Urgency bar at top: Creates immediate engagement by surfacing time-sensitive legislative actions. Subtle pulsing animation draws attention without being distracting.
  • Animated counters in hero: Social proof that the movement is large and active. Numbers create credibility and momentum.
  • Live activity feed: Shows real-time participation from other members, creating FOMO and demonstrating the platform is alive. Builds community feeling.
  • Scorecard spotlight (Champions vs Needs Improvement): Makes accountability visible on the homepage. Color-coded bars allow instant comprehension of legislator performance.
  • Campaign progress bars: Gamification element showing collective progress toward action goals. Creates urgency ("only 188 more needed").
  • Two CTAs in hero (red + gold): Red for immediate action (existing intent), gold for joining (new members). Separates conversion funnels visually.
  • Dark join CTA section with avatar stack: Social proof pattern borrowed from SaaS best practices. Humanizes the membership count.
  • Footer organized by intent: Four columns map to user goals -- learn, act, connect, support. Every link earns its place.
