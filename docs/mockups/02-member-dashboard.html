Design Decisions

  • Impact summary cards at top: Immediately reinforces the member's personal impact. "You've done this" creates pride and motivation to do more. The A- grade gamifies the experience.
  • Urgent actions with deadlines: Surfaces the most time-sensitive items first. Red left border and countdown create urgency without overwhelming. Personalized ("Your rep in TX-14 hasn't heard from you").
  • Color-coded rep scores: Green/orange/red bars allow instant visual assessment. Members can quickly identify which of their reps need the most pressure.
  • Activity timeline with dot icons: Creates a visual history of engagement. Different colors for different action types (blue=contact, gold=donate, green=event, red=campaign). Builds a sense of accomplishment over time.
  • Membership card design: Premium gradient card treatment elevates the membership experience. Shows expiration to encourage renewal. Tier name creates aspiration to upgrade.
  • Two-column layout for timeline + card: Efficient use of space. The membership card is a fixed visual anchor while the timeline can grow indefinitely.
